Data Visualization

Displaying patterns in data, emphasizing aspects we think are important, conveying our results.

Good Maps

  • Accessible by target audience.
  • Clean and easy to read.

Bad Maps

  • Misses target audience.
  • Cluttered and ineffective.

Design Principals

Gives the viewer the greatest number of ideas in the shortest time with the least ink in the smallest space.

  • Complex ideas communicated with clarity, precision, and efficiency.
  • Maximize data-to-ink ratio (i.e. more data, less other stuff).
  • Erase non-data ink, within reason
